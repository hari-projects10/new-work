TastyWorld - Food Delivery Website

Customer: Saji John

Location: Edleston, United Kingdom

Project Description

TastyWorld is a cutting-edge food delivery platform tailored specifically for the esteemed Tasty World Hotel. This website offers a seamless experience for customers to browse menus, place orders, and enjoy prompt delivery or pickup services.

Key Features

  • Menu Browsing: Explore a wide variety of dishes with detailed descriptions and prices.
  • Secure Payments: Integrated with Stripe for online payments, along with a Cash on Delivery (COD) option.
  • Order Management: Real-time order tracking and status updates.
  • Admin Panel: Manage orders, update menus, and track customer feedback with ease.
  • Responsive Design: Optimized for both desktop and mobile users for a seamless experience.

This platform is designed to enhance the dining experience by providing convenient access to delicious meals from Tasty World Hotel at the click of a button.
